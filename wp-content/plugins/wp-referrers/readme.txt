== Description ==

You can now get all the information about how people arrive at your site. WP-Referrers tracks all the referrals leading to your site, organize and neatly present them in a number of ways. To start with, you can check out the lastest referrers to your site. You can view the referrers by the referrer URL or by the local page referred to. In addition, WP-Referrers also recognizes referrals from major search engines. You can see how many hits you get from each search engine, and what keywords people use to get to your site.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the directory `wp-referrers` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Done. WP-Referrers will take care of the rest.
